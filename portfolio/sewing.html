Sewing

While my sewing experience is more limited than my crocheting and knitting abilities, I still strive for the best work I can do. I mostly only sew for myself but am more than capable of drafting custom-fit patterns for others as well as sewing said patterns.

Why I Sew

I sew because I got sick and tired of loving the fashions from the 1930-40s but not having access to the specific styles I wanted. Now I can make whatever vintage dress I want!
