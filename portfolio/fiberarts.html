Fiber Arts

As I've already mentioned, the craft I shine the most with is crocheting, and a large part of that is in my work of designing crochet patterns. I strive for my patterns to be the very best I can make them. This is why I take the time to include detailed instructions, check boxes for convenience, and notes that specify when a certain technique is being used(such as when to use yarn-over or yarn-under, the type of join to use at the end of a round, or whether or not turning chains are included in the final stitch count).
